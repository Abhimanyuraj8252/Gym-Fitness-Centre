PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: 2025-01-01

1. Data Collection

We collect basic information such as your name and physique goals to generate your Digital ID. We do not store this data on our servers; it is encoded directly into your unique URL.

2. Cloudinary Uploads

Images and Music uploaded by the Admin are hosted on Cloudinary. These assets are public if the unique link is shared.

3. Security

We use client-side hashing for the Admin Panel. While secure for casual use, please do not use sensitive real-world passwords.
